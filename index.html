Questions

Good to know

What exactly is it?
A traditional Thai herbal aroma inhaler — a small jar of natural aromatic botanicals (menthol crystals, clove, nutmeg/mace flower, pikul blossom and other Thai herbs). You open it and breathe in the cool aroma. It's an aromatherapy product, not a medicine.
What does it smell like?
Cool, sharp and crisp — bright menthol up front with warm clove and herbal notes underneath. Most people describe the first breath as "a splash of cold fresh air."
Is there any caffeine?
None. Nothing to swallow at all — it's purely an aroma you inhale. No caffeine, no sugar, no calories.
How long does one jar last?
The 10g jar lasts most people about two months with daily use. Keep it capped tightly between uses to preserve the aroma.
Where is it made?
Made in Thailand to PIC/S GMP standards — the same quality benchmark used for pharmaceuticals — and shipped to you from the US.
Any safety notes?
For inhalation of the aroma only — don't eat it or get it in your eyes. Keep away from children. Discontinue if you notice irritation. If you're pregnant or have a respiratory condition, check with your doctor first. Contains menthol.
